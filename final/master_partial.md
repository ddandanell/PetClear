# PART 1: Brand Positioning & Website Architecture

## 1.1 Brand Identity
**Brand Name:** PetClear (recommended from brand strategy)
**Tagline:** Your pet's journey, made clear.
**Brand Promise:** We make your pet's relocation to or from Dubai transparent, safe, and simple — with one WhatsApp contact and no hidden fees.

## 1.2 Brand Voice & Tone
- **Honest:** We tell the truth about costs, partners, and risks.
- **Calm:** We reduce anxiety, not create it.
- **Capable:** We demonstrate expertise without jargon.
- **Human:** We speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporation.
- **Plain-Spoken:** Simple words. Specific numbers. No fluff.

## 1.3 Value Proposition
**Primary:** Instant, transparent cost breakdowns + coordinated partner matching + WhatsApp updates at every step.

**Supporting:**
- One point of contact for a multi-vendor process
- Price protection and no hidden fees
- Document review and eligibility checks
- Real-time travel updates via WhatsApp
- Vetted, certified partners only (IPATA, IATA, MOCCAE-licensed)

## 1.4 Website Architecture
The full architecture specification is detailed in the strategy file. Key decisions:

- **Hub-spoke structure:** `/guides/` for informational content, `/routes/` for transactional content
- **Mobile-first:** Bottom-sheet navigation, thumb-zone CTAs, scroll-optimized layouts
- **Intent-based:** Pages organized by customer question, not by service category
- **WhatsApp-native:** Every page has multiple WhatsApp CTA placements
- **Scalable:** Templates ready for programmatic SEO expansion
